App Store Description

Dominoes Quest is a relaxing single-player domino puzzle game built for quick sessions and satisfying strategy. Place tiles by matching the open ends, draw when you need a better option, and try to clear your hand before the AI does.

Explore 50 progressively harder levels, collect stars, and improve your score as each board asks for smarter tile choices. With smooth touch controls, warm table visuals, and classic domino rules, Dominoes Quest is easy to start and rewarding to master.
