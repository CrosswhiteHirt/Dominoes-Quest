Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dominoes Quest handles information when you use the mobile game. Dominoes Quest is a single-player domino puzzle game. The game does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other direct contact information.

Information We Process

The game may process limited technical information needed to operate the app, save local progress, improve stability, and support advertising. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, approximate region, gameplay events, crash or diagnostic information, advertising identifiers when permitted by the user, and network-related technical data.

Local Game Progress

Dominoes Quest stores game progress, level completion, stars, and sound settings locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the game experience and does not require an account.

Advertising And Tracking Permission

The game may show ads through third-party advertising platforms. Where required by iOS, the app will request permission before accessing the device advertising identifier for tracking or personalized advertising. If you decline tracking permission, ads may still be shown, but they may be less relevant.

Third-Party Services

Dominoes Quest may use third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, security, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and app operations. These third parties may collect or receive technical data as described in their own privacy policies. The app may also request advertising configuration from a remote server to determine available ad placements.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to provide gameplay features, store progress, load and display ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud or abuse, diagnose crashes, maintain security, and improve the app experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited technical and advertising-related information may be shared with service providers and advertising partners only as needed for the purposes described in this policy, subject to their own privacy practices and applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Dominoes Quest is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete the app or reset progress inside the game. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to those services' policies and applicable legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also delete the app to remove locally stored game progress from your device. Some advertising or analytics controls may be available through your device settings or the relevant third-party provider.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no mobile app, network transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
